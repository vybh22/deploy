About EXCESSBITE

Building bridges between surplus food and communities in need

Our Mission

EXCESSBITE is a digital platform dedicated to reducing food waste by creating a seamless connection between food donors and NGOs. We believe that surplus food from restaurants, hotels, and other establishments should never go to waste when there are communities in need.

What We Do

  • Enable donors to list surplus food from their establishments
  • Connect NGOs with available food donations
  • Coordinate logistics for timely food delivery
  • Track the entire delivery process from pickup to distribution
  • Measure the environmental and social impact of our work
🌍

Environment

Every meal saved from waste reduces carbon emissions and helps build a sustainable future.

🤝

Community

We ensure underprivileged communities have access to nutritious food regardless of their economic situation.
