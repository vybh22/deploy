How It Works

A simple 6-step journey from excess to access

1

🍽️ Donor Lists Food

Restaurants, hotels, and other food donors register on EXCESSBITE and list their surplus food with details about quantity, expiry time, and pickup location.

2

✅ Admin Approval

Our admin team verifies the food listing and approves it to ensure quality and safety standards are met.

3

❤️ NGO Requests

NGOs browse available food and submit requests for the donations that match their community's needs.

4

🚚 Logistics Assigned

Our admin assigns logistics volunteers to coordinate pickup and delivery of the approved requests.

5

📍 Pickup & Delivery

Logistics volunteers pick up food from donors and deliver it to NGOs, updating the status at each step.

6

📊 Feedback & Impact

All parties provide feedback, and we track the environmental and social impact of each delivery.
